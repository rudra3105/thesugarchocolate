About Us

The story behind every sweet creation

Meet Shreya Soni

Founder & Chocolatier

What started as a passion for creating delightful desserts has blossomed into The Sugar Chocolate, a home-based bakery dedicated to crafting exceptional handmade chocolates and treats.

With years of experience and an unwavering commitment to quality, Shreya personally oversees every creation that leaves the kitchen. Each chocolate truffle, cake, and dessert is made with premium ingredients, careful attention to detail, and a genuine love for the craft.

From experimenting with unique flavor combinations to perfecting classic recipes, every product reflects a dedication to excellence and the joy of bringing happiness through chocolate.
